Available Job Positions

1. Cashier

Job Summary: Responsible for handling cash transactions, ensuring accuracy, and providing excellent customer service.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Process payments and issue receipts.
  • Maintain accurate cash drawer balances.
  • Assist customers with inquiries.

Requirements for Appointment:

  • KCSE Certificate.
  • Certificate of good conduct.
  • Computer Certificate (Basic computer and numeracy skills)

Skills & Competencies Required: Accuracy, honesty, and good customer service.
Salary: Ksh 25,000 monthly.

2. Store Clerk / Shelf Stocker

Job Summary: Receive, replenish and organize stock on shelves; ensure products are displayed correctly.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Unload deliveries and restock shelves.
  • Check expiry dates and rotate stock (FIFO).
  • Maintain clean and orderly aisles and displays.

Requirements for Appointment:

  • KCSE Certificate.
  • Physical stamina for lifting/stocking.
  • Certificate of good conduct.

Skills & Competencies Required: Organisation, reliability, teamwork.
Salary: Ksh 27,000 monthly.

3. Supervisor

Job Summary: Oversee a department or shift, manage staff, and ensure smooth operations.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Supervise and coach store staff.
  • Monitor stock levels and customer service standards.
  • Resolve customer complaints and operational issues.

Requirements for Appointment:

  • Diploma or relevant certificate; 2+ years retail supervisory experience preferred.
  • Certificate of good conduct.
  • Computer Certificate (Basic computer and numeracy skills)

Skills & Competencies Required: Leadership, organisation, communication.
Salary: Ksh 50,000 monthly.

4. Cleaner / Janitor

Job Summary: Maintain hygiene and cleanliness across the store, back-of-house and facilities.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Sweep, mop, clean restrooms and waste disposal.
  • Ensure hygiene standards and report maintenance needs.

Requirements for Appointment:

  • KCSE Certificate preferred.
  • Experience in cleaning or housekeeping advantageous.
  • Certificate of good conduct.

Skills & Competencies Required: Reliability, attention to detail, time management.
Salary: Ksh 20,000 monthly.

5. Security Officer

Job Summary: Protect store assets, staff and customers through active surveillance and security procedures.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Patrol premises, monitor CCTV and control entry/exit.
  • Prevent loss, deter theft and respond to incidents.

Requirements for Appointment:

  • KCSE Certificate and recognised security training.
  • Certificate of good conduct.

Skills & Competencies Required: Vigilance, integrity, communication.
Salary: Ksh 28,000 monthly.

6. Butcher / Meat Attendant

Job Summary: Prepare, portion and display meat products; ensure hygiene and food safety.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Cut and portion meat, maintain display and stock rotation.
  • Ensure sanitation and comply with food safety standards.

Requirements for Appointment:

  • Experience in butchery or food handling; food hygiene certificate preferred.
  • Certificate of good conduct.

Skills & Competencies Required: Food safety knowledge, manual dexterity, customer service.
Salary: Ksh 35,000 monthly.

7. Baker / Pastry Assistant

Job Summary: Assist in preparing baked goods, maintain bakery section and ensure product quality.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Mix, bake, and finish bakery items according to recipes.

Requirements for Appointment:

  • Certificate of good conduct.

  • Process invoices, reconcile accounts and assist with month-end closing.

  • Certificate of good conduct.
